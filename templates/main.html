Andrew Krufka

Data Analyst

The son of a professional photographer and a real estate agent, he got his start assisting his father on photo shoots for his own projects. That quickly led to commissions from their friends and before he graduated from high school he was already steadily in demand in Philadelphia’s vibrant creative community.

A move to Orlando for film school led to contacts in the entertainment and advertising worlds. Upon graduating, he worked on many big budget television and films in the Atlanta Metro Area as a member of the grip department, while being in the IATSE 479 union. After a few years of working in the entertainment industry, Andrew’s began to revisit his old passion for photography, and ended up becoming addicted to working in photoshop and creating photoshop actions.

Andrew has been honored to work with top international brands and companies, as well as leading figures in the entertainment industry. He is always excited when projects allow him to meet new people and travel to new places. Andrew has worked both in-house and as a freelancer, with many well known brands, such as: Balenciaga, Burberry, Chloe, Coach, Dolce & Gabbana, Gucci, Jimmy Choo, Kate Spade New York, Lord & Taylor, Saks Fifth Avenue, Saks off Fifth, and Tom Ford to name a few.
